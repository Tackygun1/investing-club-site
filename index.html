How to get started in 3 steps

You don’t need any experience or money. Just curiosity, basic respect for others, and a willingness to learn.

  1. Fill out the member interest form.
  2. Show up to a meeting (time & room listed above). Bring a laptop if you have one.
  3. Join our announcements channel: [Remind / email / Discord invite here].

We never ask members to trade real money. All competitions are run with virtual portfolios or purely hypothetical strategies, and are designed to help you think critically about risk.
